Statistical Analysis in R: A Brief Introduction

Structure of the book

Chapters 1 and 2 will cover downloading and describing R and the RStudio IDE (what even is an IDE?), and programming basics, such as assignment, data types and structures, and functions and arguments. Chapters 3 and 4 will cover installing/importing packages (what is a package and why do I need it?), importing data, and formatting the data in a way that is conducive to analysis. Chapters 5 and 6 will briefly cover descriptive statistics, quality assurance, and visualizing data. Chapters 7-13 will cover various statistical tests that all fall under the GLM umbrella.
